* I am a M.S. student in the [Computer Science Department](https://cs.illinois.edu) at [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu). During my studies, I have worked with Prof. [Tianyin Xu](https://tianyin.github.io), Prof. [Nikita Borisov](https://hatswitch.org/~nikita/) and Prof. [Josep Torrellas](http://iacoma.cs.uiuc.edu/josep/torrellas.html) over different projects.
* My research interests lie in making computer systems more secure, reliable and privacy-preserving. Particularly, I work on building privacy-preserving systems, enabling more reliable software systems and software/hardware co-design to enhance system efficiency.
* I received my Bachelor’s Degree in [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](http://en.sjtu.edu.cn/). My undergraduate research advisors are Prof. [Haojin Zhu](https://nsec.sjtu.edu.cn/~hjzhu/).
